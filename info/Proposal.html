Problem

Unemployment, this is a problem that only grows the greater society grows;
old methods serve no purpose anymore, and newer methods are taking people’s
jobs away. So what can we do? We shouldn’t look forward, we shouldn’t look
backwards, we must step sideways and think outside the box.

Strategic Goal

To help the community have access to different jobs in the main segment,
newborn students with the difficulty of finding work, facilitate the search
and the objective.

Methodology

  • Understand the problem by contacting people who either live it or see it every day.
  • Ask for what the client(s) would like the web page to have. (Requirements).
  • Make prototypical designs following carefully the requirements. (Software Design).
  • Get validation of the best prototype by the client or go back to step three. (Design Validation).
  • Start developing back-end (Source Code).
  • Work out the functions the user will be able to access (front-end) (Integration).
  • Lookout for errors in the system and correcting them (Debugging).
  • Show software to client and explain how it works and how he can control it.
    If he wants a slight change, go back to step 5. (Validation).
  • Give product to client (Delivery).
